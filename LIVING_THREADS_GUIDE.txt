Navigator v3.3 — Living Threads Guide

Living Threads are ongoing coherence commitments. They are not ordinary tasks or productivity goals.

A thread can hold:
- a practice such as walking, writing, prayer, music, reading, sleep, or recovery;
- a Beauty/Awe source such as nature, art, gratitude, sacredness, humor, or wonder;
- a fear wall or blocked agency threshold;
- an inquiry in philosophy, science, research, learning, or meaning-making;
- a relationship, repair, BioBond, or support concern;
- a career or identity-protection concern.

Suggested test path:
1. Open Threads.
2. Create one Practice Thread with title, why it matters, obstacle, small next contact, and one connected coherence signal.
3. Confirm it appears on Threads and Home.
4. Edit the thread and save.
5. Pause it, resume it, and complete it.
6. Ask Twin about the thread; confirm the Twin input is prefilled and the Twin workspace opens.
7. Convert the thread into a Move Card; confirm it appears in Moves.
8. Export JSON and confirm livingThreads appears in the file.
9. Restore or merge JSON and confirm the thread survives.

Design rule:
The Twin should remember, invite, and help revise Living Threads. It should not nag, shame, or reduce the user to a checklist.
